Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Parlami.ai ("Parlami," "we," "us") provides AI-assisted marketing services for businesses. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you submit through our demo request form: name, business name, email, phone, and website.
  • Marketing account data you authorize when you become a client — e.g., Google Ads, Google Analytics, Google Business Profile, and Meta (Facebook/Instagram) data connected via OAuth. We never ask for, or store, your account passwords.
  • Website usage data such as standard server logs from our hosting providers.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to demo requests and provide the services you've engaged us for.
  • To monitor, analyze, and report on your marketing performance through our AI agents.
  • To recommend and — only with your approval — execute marketing actions on your connected accounts.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share client marketing data with other clients. Credentials and data are isolated per client.
  • We do not make changes to your accounts without your approval.

Security

API credentials are encrypted at rest and stored on hardened infrastructure with firewall protection, key-only access, and brute-force blocking. Account connections use OAuth, which you can revoke at any time from your Google or Meta account settings.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain client data only as long as needed to provide the service. When an engagement ends — or at any time on request — we will delete your connected account data and revoke our own access. Email info@parlami.ai to request deletion.

Third-Party Services

Our website and services rely on third-party processors including hosting providers (Vercel, Render), Google APIs, and Meta APIs. Form submissions are delivered to us by a form-forwarding service. Each processor handles data under its own privacy terms.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing info@parlami.ai.

Contact

Questions about this policy: info@parlami.ai
