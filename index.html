Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about how an AI marketing team actually works.

Is Parlami.ai fully automated, or is there a human involved?
Both. The 12 AI agents monitor and analyze 24/7, but nothing executes without approval. Every client also gets a dedicated human account manager who reviews the daily briefings and helps you make major decisions.
Do I have to hand over my account passwords?
No. Google Ads, Analytics, and social accounts connect through OAuth — you grant access without ever sharing a password, and you can revoke it anytime. All credentials are encrypted at rest and isolated per client.
How is this different from hiring an agency?
A typical agency costs $5K–$15K/month and checks your account a few times a week. Parlami's agents check it around the clock, flag problems by urgency the moment they appear, and deliver one consolidated morning briefing — starting at $997/month.
What kinds of businesses does Parlami work with?
Any local or lead-driven business: schools, law firms, home services, healthcare, real estate, and more. The agent team is configured to your business, your geography, and your goals — it was born running marketing for two real preschools.
Can the AI agents make changes without my permission?
No. Agents recommend; you approve. Every recommendation comes with evidence and expected impact, and every executed action is logged. You stay in control, always.
How fast can I get started?
After a demo call, your agent team is configured on your business data and starts monitoring within days. You'll see your first morning briefing the next business day after activation.
