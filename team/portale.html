What Portale Does For You

Portale finds where people give up. He watches the path from ad click to submitted form and pinpoints the dead forms, leaky landing pages, and abandoned sign-ups that waste traffic you already paid for.

Key Capabilities

  • Measures where form-fillers quit, field by field, when the data supports it

  • Recommends the cheapest fix first — repair the funnel before buying more clicks
